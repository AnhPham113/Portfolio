a bit about me

I’m Trung Anh. I graduated with a degree in Biotechnology from the University of Science, but soon realized that the field didn’t truly fit me. That realization encouraged me to explore different paths in search of something more aligned with who I am.

I went on to work as an English teaching assistant, an academic officer, and later as a math teacher. During that time, some of my tasks required design-related work, which I first learned on my own through online resources. Curiosity led me to enroll in a design course, where I was introduced to UI/UX. By chance, I also had the opportunity to redesign the website and app for the center where I had once taught. Drawing on both my teaching background and my growing knowledge of UI/UX, I helped the center address long-standing issues in their digital platforms.

After completing two real-world projects, I finally found what I had been searching for. UI/UX design combines creativity, logic, and structure in a way that feels natural to me. I enjoy exploring simple solutions to challenges, and since then I’ve been committed to pursuing this field with focus and sustainability.
