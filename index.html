a bit about me

I’m Trung Anh. I graduated with a degree in Biotechnology from the University of Science, but soon realized that the field didn’t quite fit me. That realization pushed me to explore different paths, hoping to find something more aligned with who I am.

I’ve worked as an English teaching assistant, an academic officer, and eventually, a math teacher. While working in education, I came across design tools to support my tasks—and that’s how I first discovered UI/UX. I was curious, so I decided to enroll in a design course to dive deeper.

After completing two real-world projects, I found what I had been searching for. UI/UX design brings together creativity, logic, and structure in a way that just clicks with me. Since then, I’ve been committed to growing in this field seriously and sustainably.
